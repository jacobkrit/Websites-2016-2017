Rosy Ipostatiko(2 – 3 sleeps)

Those picturesque cottages used to be an inextricable part of the complex, because at the time when Georgilas Cave House was a lively farm, “Ipostatika” were serving as warehouses for the storage of several cereals, flour, oil, wine and “strofilia” (the local drink made from the grape skins). What is more, their northern-west position was ensuring a steadily cool temperature ideal for the natural refrigeration of fresh fruits, vegetables and cheese produced from the fields under Georgilas Cave House.

Notwithstanding, following an attentive restoration in 2013, “Ipostatika” can ideally host couples, which seek for romance and relaxation, as well as young parents with one small child!

More about Rosy

-Rosy-

Into an area of 40m2 there is an extra – large built double-bed, a baby – cot, a spacious living room, an equipped kitchenette, a spacious bathroom in “rosé” colouring, with built shower and double wash-basin. The decoration is mainly rustic with antiques and handmade furniture. The private terrace has direct access to the pool and the garden, as well as panoramic view to Eggares Valley.
