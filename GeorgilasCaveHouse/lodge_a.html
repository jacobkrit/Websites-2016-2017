“Archontiko” is the flagship domicile of the complex. It’s been always open to guests either for fetes or religious celebrations or other formal occasions!

More about Arcontiko

-ARCHONTIKO MANSION -

Its construction was based on the dictates of climate, the particular housing requirements and the materials available to the local masons. In particular, the build is rising in three levels, communicating through stone staircases: the individual utility-room, named “Loutro” (first level), the main house (second level) and the attic – office (third level).

The second and the third level are nestled at an elevated position and, but for their earth-toned colouring, they are well secluded and not easily visible from afar, ensuring thus privacy, calmness and direct contact with nature!

The build’s interior is combining a blend of comfort and traditional luxury: “Archontiko” comprises 3 high ceiling bedrooms, with en-suite bathroom, wardrobe and private entrance each, as well as a spacious lobby, a fully equipped kitchen, a spiral staircase leading to the attic, and two large private verandas with pool - garden view, and panoramic view to the valley.

The decoration is mainly rustic and elegant with antiques and handmade furniture. “Archontiko” has, of course, direct access to the 1 acre pebbled garden and the 5 acre citrus grove.

“Archontiko” was recently refurbished and converted into an impressive mansion, which is able to accommodate comfortably large groups of guests.
